Allosaurus

Allosaurus lived in Yakutia in the early Cretaceous period. He had a long skull with pointed teeth. Allosaurus fed on herbivorous dinosaurs such as Stegosaurus, Camarasaurus and other animals of that time. Fragments of Allosaurus were found in the area of Suntarsky ulus by an expedition with Suntar schoolchildren.
