Prehistoric Yakutia

In "Prehistoric Yakutia" you can learn about the prehistoric fauna of Yakutia. Despite the fact that our republic has established itself as the main region for finding mammoth fossils, dinosaurs and other organisms lived on the Yakut land in the Mesozoic era. On this site you can learn the history of studying the prehistoric fauna of Yakutia and see photos of the founded fossils.

The site is currently under development. In the future, it is planned to expand the content of the site: to develop mini quizzes and game familiarisation with science to attract younger generations, to expand the variety of articles and their content for teenagers and adults.
